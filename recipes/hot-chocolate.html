Hot Chocolate

Description

Can't beat a cup of cocoa on a cold winter's day.

And you can't beat a cup of cocoa made in a Velvetiser.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate flakes
  • Milk

Method

  1. Pour the milk in up to the line.
  2. Add a scoop of chocolate flakes.
  3. Turn on the Velvetiser and wait for the golden syrup of wonder.
