Lasagne

Description

Unimaginative, I know.

Time limited, focusing on the coding not the content.

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Pasta sheets

Method

  1. Brown the onions
  2. Add the mince
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, herbs etc etc and simmer for 45 minutes
  4. Layer sauce then pasta sheet, and repeat twice
  5. Pour white sauce over the lasagne and bake for half an hour
